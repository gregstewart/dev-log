Fusebox 5 Skeleton Application

Install the Fusebox 5 core files (see the Downloads section of fusebox.org).

Copy this (skeleton) directory into your webroot.

Test everything works by hitting this URL in your browser:

	http://localhost/skeleton/
	
Replace "localhost" with whatever is appropriate for your server.

Please note that the lexicon/cf/ directory contains a number of verbs that
have been submitted by the Fusebox community. Not all of these verbs have
been tested by the Fusebox Corporation and they are provided as-is for your
convenience and education. Please contact the authors of the verbs for more
information. Unattributed verbs were most likely submitted by Sean Corfield
seancorfield@gmail.com.
